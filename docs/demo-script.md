# Demo Script

This script can be used for a portfolio video, recruiter walkthrough, or client demo.

## Suggested Duration

3–5 minutes.

## Opening

"ERP SKALA UMKM is a mobile-first ERP concept for Indonesian small businesses. It helps owners and staff record sales, monitor stock, track cash movement, manage customer orders, and view payroll summaries from a single mobile app."

## Scene 1 — Login and Business Selection

Show:

- login screen,
- select business,
- dashboard landing.

Explain:

"The system supports multi-business access. A user can belong to more than one business, and the selected business context controls the data shown in the app."

## Scene 2 — Dashboard

Show:

- cash summary,
- sales summary,
- inventory summary,
- receivable/payable signal.

Explain:

"The dashboard focuses on simple operational indicators instead of overwhelming accounting reports."

## Scene 3 — POS Transaction

Show:

- product list,
- search/filter,
- add product to cart,
- customer selection,
- payment method selection,
- save draft,
- post sale,
- receipt.

Explain:

"A sale starts as a draft and becomes official after posting. Posting affects sales history, stock, and financial records."

## Scene 4 — Inventory

Show:

- product list,
- stock position,
- stock movement,
- low stock or out-of-stock indicator.

Explain:

"Inventory is not only a product list. It also shows stock movement so business owners can understand why stock changed."

## Scene 5 — Finance

Show:

- cash and bank summary,
- cash book,
- income/expense,
- simple profit/loss.

Explain:

"The finance module uses simpler language for business owners while the backend still keeps structured accounting logic."

## Scene 6 — CRM Order

Show:

- customer order,
- down payment,
- order detail,
- conversion to sale.

Explain:

"Customer orders and down payments are handled before they become final sales."

## Scene 7 — HRM & Payroll

Show:

- employee/payroll summary,
- payroll run detail,
- salary payment summary.

Explain:

"Payroll is connected to finance so salary payment is part of business cash movement."

## Closing

"This project demonstrates full-stack product execution: Flutter mobile app, Laravel API, multi-tenant business logic, finance and inventory workflows, and practical documentation for a real MSME use case."
